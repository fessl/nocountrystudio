I’m a writer with a strategic mind and aesthetic eye.

I believe in the fusion of writing and design to communicate ideas. One coin. Words are the raw materials of my craft, and design — a storytelling tool.

Perhaps my portfolio makes it more clear.

Day to day I run No Country Studio – making companies sound more human through human-centric copywriting. Get in touch to see how I can help elevate your voice and editorial efforts.

In the past, I spent a few years managing the content production process at Skyrocket. I also worked in startups, ecommerce, web publishing, web design and social media.

Besides work, I read and review books. Occasionally I write short stories. Even more occasionally — essays.

My formal education was in marketing at Thompson Rivers University and international business at Université Catholique de Lyon. But most of what I've remembered I learned on my own.

I live in Vancouver, BC.

I work worldwide.
